Rangamati:

Rangamati, known as the "city of lakes", is a popular tourist destination in Bangladesh, particularly for its natural beauty and the iconic Kaptai Lake. It offers a mix of attractions including natural scenery, cultural sites, and historical landmarks, making it a diverse travel destination. You can go to Baghaichhari from Rangamati by boat or by road. Launches leave from Reserve Bazar launch pier every day between 7.30 and 10.30 am. The fare is 140-200 taka per person. It takes 5-6 hours. Buses leave from the bus terminal between 7.30 and 8.30 am, the fare is 200 taka per person. It takes 6-7 hours. You can also go to Baghaichhari directly from Chittagong or via Khagrachari. You can also go to Baghaichhari directly from Dhaka.
